When Should We Go?

Fall, Winter
or Spring?

Each season offers a completely different China. Here's what Alison & Eric can expect across each window.

Eric's Pick October – November

Fall 2026

★★★★★ — Best Overall

Ideal conditions everywhere. Zhangjiajie turns golden-red, the Li River is crystal clear, Xi'an is crisp and pleasant. Avoid Golden Week (Oct 1–7) — aim for mid-October to mid-November. Temperatures: 12–22°C. Peak season, peak beauty.

December – February

Winter 2026–27

★★★☆☆ — Budget Pick

Fewer crowds, noticeably lower prices. Chengdu stays mild (8–14°C). Guilin is cool but manageable. Zhangjiajie in winter mist is stunning for photos. Beijing gets cold (-5 to 3°C). Best for travellers happy to layer up and save money.

March – May

Spring 2027

★★★★☆ — Romantic Alternative

Blooming landscapes and warming temps. Guilin turns lush green. Zhangjiajie is wrapped in morning mist. Chengdu cherry blossoms peak in March. Temperatures 14–24°C by May. Light rain possible in Guilin — pack a layer.

Note from Eric

Fall 2026 (mid-October) is the sweet spot — perfect weather everywhere, spectacular autumn colours in Zhangjiajie, and no Golden Week crowds. That said, Spring 2027 would be incredibly romantic if timing doesn't work out for fall.

Before We Go

Key Logistics

Everything to sort before departure from Toronto.

📋

Visa

Canadian citizens need a Chinese tourist visa (L-visa). Apply at the Chinese Consulate in Toronto or via a visa agent. Allow 4–6 weeks. Fee ~$80–100 CAD. Requires passport photos and a rough itinerary.

💳

Money & Payments

China is nearly cashless. Set up WeChat Pay or Alipay linked to a Canadian card before leaving. Withdraw some Chinese Yuan (CNY) on arrival for small vendors and markets.

📱

VPN & Internet

Google, Instagram, and WhatsApp are blocked. Download ExpressVPN or NordVPN before leaving Canada. Get a China SIM card at the airport on arrival (China Mobile or China Unicom).

🚄

Getting Around

China's high-speed rail is world-class — fast, affordable, always on time. Book via Trip.com (easiest for foreigners). City metro systems are excellent and very inexpensive.

🏥

Health & Insurance

Get comprehensive travel insurance including medical evacuation (Blue Cross, Manulife). Hepatitis A/B and Typhoid vaccinations recommended. Bring all prescription medications from home.

✈️

Flights from Toronto

Air Canada or China Eastern via Vancouver or Shanghai into Chengdu (CDU). Return from Guilin (KWL) or Beijing (PEK). Book 3–4 months early for best fares — set a Google Flights alert now.
